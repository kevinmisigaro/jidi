Capability Statement

OVERVIEW

JAMII INTEGRATED DEVELOPMENT INITIATIVE COMPANY LIMITED (jidi) OFFERS PROFESSIONAL ADVISORY SERVICES TO BUILD INCLUSIVE SYSTEMS FOR ENHANCING OUTCOME AND IMPACT ORIENTED PLANNING, MONITORING AND EVALUATION (OIO-PME) FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT.

We apply a system-level approach to assess on the-ground realities, jointly develop solutions to system barriers, and provide operational advise improved project and/ or program implementation efficiencies. We work with our partners to increase knowledge and capacity of state and non-state entities to implement inclusive development strategies that leverage resources and mitigate risks. With offices in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania and network of senior consultants and leading experts located within Tanzania, East Africa (…with strategic engagement partnership throughout the world), jidi provided partners with responsive and tailored services.

OUR SERVICES

jidi partners with and advises state and non-state players to address development challenges. We work with our partners to create innovative solutions for enhancing project OIO-PME, conduct capacity building, research and documentation, and foster effective networking and collaborations.


Enhancing project OIO-PME

Transforming reports from output to outcome and impact oriented reports; we expedite review of the existing Monitoring, Evaluation and Learning (MEL) frameworks, work together to develop participatory MEL and orient on the step by step procedures for data collection tools and methods to fit monitoring purposes.


Training and capacity building

We increase the knowledge and capacity of state and non-state partners to implement inclusive development strategies that leverage resources and mitigate risks. We offer tailored training and capacity building services to meet the needs of government, donors, practitioners and technological requirements for improved efficiency, effectiveness and equity.


Research and documentation

We design and lead small-and large-scale social research interventions to answer questions that help our partner better understand how their projects and programs are performing and how we can improve systems and strategies for improved efficiency, effectiveness and equity. In addition, we support our partners with systematic documentation of the project and programs processes and discern lessons for improved programming as well as sharing with wider stakeholders.

OUR EXPERTISE

  • • Overall organisation development support

About jidi


Jamii Integrated Development Initiatives (jidi) is a Tanzania based registered firm committed to building holistic sustainable development. We work with a unique and multi-sectoral network of institutions, individual experts and volunteers; servicing non-profit, government and donors with innovative solutions within the development context.

Why Jidi


The firm brings together a team of professionals with more than 15 years of progressive experience on a wide range of projects and initiatives in health care, nutrition, agriculture, education and livelihoods. The coming together of the local experts brings synergy of their different expertise and experience that is instrumental at delivering exceptional value to our clients.

Mission

We work to foster broad-based collaborative initiatives for delivering exceptional value to our clients, to fulfil ourselves and to make a meaningful contribution to the development sector in Tanzania




Vision

Community that seeks at integrating and operationalising global perspectives into local community-centred solutions in promoting holistic sustainable development. This is achieved through optimizing quality of provided services, continuous monitoring and follow up and nurturing of the faculty of comprehension of the requirements and needs of our clients.

Business Goal

Looking at the future, we are highly confident that jidi has the team with strong background and the appropriate vision of success but also flexible to allow it to continue its development and proactively face the challenges of the new era.



Our Approach


Our approach to socio-economic development is informed by decades of experience of the team members bringing the right people together to facilitate the exchange of knowledge and skills necessary to meet global development challenges including poverty, access to social services, nutrition and agriculture, and community engagement (with special focus to youth and adolescents).
